Outreach

Book about evolution

I find teaching, mentoring and outreach activities rewarding and important aspects of my work. I have authored a chapter of a book aimed to communicate selected topics of evolutionary biology to high school students, which is about to be published in May 2026.

Hosting high-school students

As part of an outreach project, I hosted yearly visits of high school students at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria, and organized laboratory tours for them. As part of their visit, I presented them about the research topics of the Vicoso group, such as the evolution of sex, sexual dimorphism, and what we can learn about their evolutionary impacts when we study asexual species, like brine shrimp (Artemia clade).
